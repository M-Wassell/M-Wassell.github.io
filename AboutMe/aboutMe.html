About Me

Intoduction

I am a .NET developer currently working within the NHS, building and maintaining internal applications used by clinical departments including Cardiothoracic and Vascular teams. My work focuses on developing reliable, maintainable software using C#, .NET, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and relational databases. I enjoy translating real-world operational problems into practical software solutions. My background across development, IT consulting, and customer-facing technical roles gives me a strong ability to understand business requirements and deliver systems that are both technically robust and aligned with user needs. I am particularly interested in building well-structured applications using clean code principles, object-oriented design, and maintainable architectures.

Current Role

I currently work as a Systems Developer within the NHS, where I design, build and maintain internal applications that support clinical and operational workflows. My role involves analysing business requirements from clinical departments and translating them into technical designs and working software solutions. These systems are used by staff in cardiothoracic and vascular services, so reliability, usability, and compliance with national healthcare IT standards are critical. I work closely with stakeholders, clinicians and internal IT teams to ensure applications support real operational needs while aligning with wider NHS IM&T strategies and security standards.
